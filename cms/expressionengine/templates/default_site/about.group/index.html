About

On Plate, Still Hungry is a food, drink, travel and culture magazine that sees a worldwide collective of writers, artists, chefs, designers, photographers, musicians and filmmakers share meals, stories, interviews and ideas on the daily, family styles.

From London to Los Angeles, New York to Buenos Aires, Shanghai, Sao Paulo, Manila, Rome, Tokyo and more, On Plate, Still Hungry serves up a constantly growing creative community with a worldwide hunger for modern food culture delivered with a fresh, new voice.

On Plate, Still Hungry is co-founded by Kat Popiel and Terence The with Max Spencer as creative director and Carlos Carneiro as producer and curator of On Plate Cinema.
